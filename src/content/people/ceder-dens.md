I currently work on deep learning methods for _de novo_ peptide sequencing, with a primary focus on immunopeptidomics and the identification of peptides beyond traditional database-driven approaches.

In the past, I contributed to improving proteomics workflows and database search strategies, addressing challenges related to data quality, handling post-translational modifications, and large-scale data analysis.

I also worked on computational immunology, where I developed and analyzed machine learning models for TCR-epitope interaction prediction. This included the use of interpretable deep learning techniques to uncover molecular binding patterns and assess model reliability, as well as the design of transformer-based architectures for paired biological sequence data.
